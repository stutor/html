Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stutor Free?

Stutor is free for everyone to use! We offer two platforms, a peer-to-peer tutoring platform and a professional tutoring system. The purpose of Stutor is for you to be able to conveniently find a tutor that would be able to best help you. Stutor is built upon a network of dedicated and committed students who are willing to devote time to tutor others. The professional platform, however, is a paid system, in which you wil receive tutoring services from someone that has been screened to be able to provide tutoring at a professional level

How will I be able to access Stutor?

You will be able to access Stutor from any device with internet access! (although tablet or computer is preferred). Currently, the mobile/tablet application has been launched for Android systems, with ongoing development for iOS and web application.

How are tutors hired by Stutor?

Peer-to-peer tutors can be anyone that is currently enrolled in high school or attend a university. Because Stutor relies upon a peer-to-peer network, Stutor will reach out to tutors in communities throughout the United States to join our network. Additionally, anyone qualified will also be able to apply to become a professional tutor.
